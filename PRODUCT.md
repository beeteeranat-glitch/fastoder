# Product

## Register

product

## Users

FastOrder is used by drink and food shop customers placing quick mobile orders, and by shop staff managing incoming orders from the admin surface during service hours.

## Product Purpose

The product lets customers browse a menu, checkout as guests or returning customers, pay by cash or transfer, track order status in realtime, and earn or redeem loyalty rewards. For staff, success means new orders are easy to notice, verify, prepare, complete, and print.

## Brand Personality

Fast, friendly, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page flourish inside task screens, over-decorated form controls, hidden order details, and checkout steps that slow down repeat customers.

## Design Principles

- Keep checkout decisions explicit and hard to misread.
- Preserve staff speed during busy shop operations.
- Make prices, payment requirements, and order status visible before confirmation.
- Prefer familiar mobile ordering patterns over novelty.
- Keep guest checkout and returning-customer flows equally clear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Thai labels, strong contrast for form states and status messages, keyboard-accessible controls, and reduced-motion-friendly interactions.
